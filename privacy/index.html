Privacy Policy

How Lauburu Grappling Map collects, uses, stores, and protects your data, plus how to request deletion.

Last updated: 2026-05-04

1. Health and fitness data

The app reads (with your explicit permission) health and fitness data from these sources:

  • Apple Health (HealthKit) — sleep, steps, heart rate, HRV, resting heart rate, active energy, workouts, distance.
  • Android Health Connect — heart rate, HRV, resting HR, sleep, steps, exercise, active calories, and historical health data.
  • WHOOP CSV imports — recoveries, sleeps, cycles, workouts that you export and import.
  • WHOOP Direct (OAuth) — recovery, sleep, cycle, strain, workouts authorised through WHOOP's API.
  • Polar Flow / Samsung Health (via Health Connect on Android) — same metric categories as above where available.
  • Cronometer (where you connect it) — calories, protein, carbs, fat.

All metric reads are initiated by you. You can revoke any platform permission at any time and the app stops reading those metrics immediately.

READ_HEALTH_DATA_HISTORY is requested so the app can build personal trend baselines (7/14/30/90-day rolling averages, variability, consistency) on first sync, after a device reset, or when you connect a new tracker. Backfills are explicitly initiated by you.

2. Bluetooth and location permissions

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to pair with FTMS-compatible bikes/rowers/ski-ergs, Concept2 PM5 monitors, Bluetooth heart-rate straps (Polar H10, H9, Verity Sense; Wahoo TICKR; Scosche Rhythm+; Garmin straps), and Echelon-branded equipment.

BLUETOOTH_SCAN, BLUETOOTH_CONNECT, ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION are used solely to discover and pair with these devices. On Android API levels below 31 the BLE stack requires ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION to scan; the app does not derive, store, or transmit your physical location.

3. Storage and processing

  • Primary storage — Railway-hosted backend. Normalised daily metrics, training sessions, AI Coach context, and feedback submissions are stored in a Postgres database on Railway, isolated per-account. Encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS) and at rest (Railway-managed encryption).
  • Secondary mirror — Supabase. Selected aggregate-only data is mirrored for redundancy and read-side performance. Row-level security ensures each user can only access their own records.
  • On-device caching. The mobile app caches your most recent metrics, training history, and preferences using OS-secure storage (Keychain / Keystore via expo-secure-store). Clearing the app or signing out removes this cache.

4. AI Coach and analysis

When you ask the AI Coach a question, the app sends a structured request containing the question you typed, your current Lauburu Readiness summary, your normalised daily metrics for the last up to 90 days, and relevant training-context fields. The AI Coach reasons over your data only — it does not blend data from other users. Aggregate or population-level insights are not enabled by default.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or for personalised ads.
  • We do not share identifiable health data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not track your physical location.

6. User controls

  • Revoke platform access — disable any source from Apple Health, Android Health Connect, WHOOP, Cronometer, or Polar settings.
  • Disconnect inside the app — Settings shows every connected source with a Disconnect action.
  • Delete your account — see the Account and Data Deletion Request page. We delete or anonymise account data within 30 days unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or payment-record reasons.
  • Export — on request we will provide a copy of your stored data (normalised metrics, training sessions, AI Coach context).

7. Security

  • HTTPS / TLS for all network requests.
  • OS-secure storage for tokens and session credentials on-device.
  • Server-side row-level security on the Supabase mirror.
  • Service-role secrets are used only for backend-to-database operations and are never exposed to the client.

8. Tester / internal-testing builds

Internal testing builds (Google Play Internal Testing, TestFlight) are restricted to invited testers only. Bug-report and feedback content submitted from inside the app may include device model, OS version, app build number, and a free-text description; these submissions are stored on the same Railway backend and are visible only to the project owner.

9. Children

Lauburu Grappling Map is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or to export your data, email support@lauburugrapplingmap.com. We reply within 30 days.
