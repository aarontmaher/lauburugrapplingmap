Human-readable companion to grappling-placement-memory.json

## Categories & Positions

### Dominant Positions (Attacker / Defender)
Back Control, Front Headlock, Mount, North South, Side Control, Turtle

### Guard (Passer / Guard player)
Butterfly ashi, Butterfly guard, Closed guard, De la riva, Half butterfly, Half guard, Half guard passing, Headquarters, J point, K guard, Knee shield half guard, Octopus guard, Outside ashi, Quarter guard, Reverse de la riva, Seated guard, Shin pin, Single leg X, Supine guard, X guard

### Scrambles (Initiative / Defensive)
Berimbolo, Crab ride, Grounded 50/50

### Wrestling
- Hand fighting (You / Opponent, 5 headings — NO Submissions): Outside tie, Inside tie, Collar tie, Underhook, Overhook, 2-on-1, Over/under, Wrist control
- Shots (Attacker / Defender): Head inside single leg, Head outside single leg, Double leg, Low ankle
- Wrestling bodylock

## Key Placement Rules

| Rule | Example |
|------|---------|
| Submissions = flat finishes | arm-bar, mounted-triangle, kimura |
| Offence = attack families/chains | top-side-crucifix/kimura, head-in-the-hole-go-behind/whip-by |
| Counter to X = other perspective | dogfight counter → passer/defence |
| Direction matters in names | throw-by-to-2-on-1 (not 2-on-1/offence) |
| Outcome folders usually unnecessary | leg-drag (not leg-drag/side-control) |
| Don't over-nest | 2-3 key branch points, not every spoken step |

## Locked Naming
- D'Arce (slug: darce)
- Von Flue
- Single arm chest wrap = control + offence
- Knee on belly = sub-position under Side Control
- Head arm choke = submission

## Perspective Distinction Rules

| Scenario | Perspective | Heading | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|---------|
| Defensive escape technique | defender | defence | wrist-control-spin-out |
| Attacking escape (you attack to get out) | defender | offence | knee-tap-fireman |
| Counter to opponent's defence | attacker | offence | limp-arm counter to shin whizzer |
| Your own defensive technique | defender | defence | shin-whizzer as defender |
| Same technique, who's doing it? | depends | depends | shin whizzer appears both sides |

**In shots:** attacker = shooter, defender = being shot on.
- "I shoot, they whizzer, I limp arm" = attacker/offence
- "They shoot, I whizzer" = defender/defence
