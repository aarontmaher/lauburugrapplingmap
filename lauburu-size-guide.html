How to Measure

Chest / Waist / Hip (half):
Lay the garment flat. Measure straight across from edge to edge at the relevant point.
Length:
Measure from the top of the waistband (bottoms) or shoulder seam (tops) to the hem.
Inseam:
Measure from the crotch seam to the bottom of the leg opening.
Sleeve:
Measure from the center back neck seam, across the shoulder, to the cuff.

All measurements are garment measurements taken laid flat. Actual product measurements may vary by up to 1" (2.5 cm) as products are custom-made by hand.
