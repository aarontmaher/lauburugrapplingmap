# PURPLE BELT SYLLABUS
Systems and chains. Built on White + Blue belt foundations.
Started: 2026-03-25

## Principles
- Systems-level understanding, not isolated techniques
- Chains with if/then branching based on opponent reactions
- No-gi only
- Tied to actual positions in the map
- ALL technique selections from Aaron's mapped content only

## Promotion Rule
To move from Purple belt to Brown belt syllabus, the student must:
- Demonstrate systematic understanding of each section in live training
- Provide video proof of chains and system application
- No exceptions

## Prerequisite
Blue belt syllabus must be completed with video proof.
